About Me

My name is Tyson Parkin and I am young professional who is currently enrolled in a Coding Bootcamp through the University of Toronto’s School of Continuing Studies. My Bachelor’s degree in Philosophy from York University fostered the critical thinking skills that allow me to learn quickly, and apply my knowledge effectively in this fast-paced industry.

It was my successful career in IT Sales that first sparked my interest in coding. My resume includes positions at many well-known and respected companies within the industry such as Softchoice, CDW and Rogers. Combined with my background in visual arts from Etobicoke School of the Arts, I am perfectly suited for a career which requires both creativity and an understanding of technology. I am always looking to learn and grow, and I am very excited to begin my career as a Web Developer here in Toronto where I live with my partner and cat (pictured). If you are a recruiter please feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn.
